CWMO Platform

It is an application that permit to get assist the management and the implementation of creative workshop. This platform aims to experiment functionalities that respond to needs of the differents roles that can be involved in the creative workshop: facilitator, project owner, organizer of the workshop, eventual experts and of course ideators who will provide there ideas.
